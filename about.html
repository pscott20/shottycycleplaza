About Us

Shotty Cycle Plaza

A motorcycle shop located in Wyandotte, Kansas. They can repair, restore, and customize vintage motorcycles. You can follow their work on their instagram, linked below. They offer a wide range of services from oil changes to valve adjustments. They focus on carburated JDM bikes. See the 'Customers' page for a full list of services.

Owner

Jordan Thomas is the owner/founder of Shotty Cycle Plaza. He has 15 years of experience fixing motorcycles, cars/trucks, houses and boats. The "Wizard of Wyandotte" has seen and fixed a lot. You can follow Jordan on his Instagram.
