Yamaha 250 Enduro

Current Project

This old Yamaha enduro came in one piece but not running. After a lot of trouble shooting we were able to get spark.

Shortly after spark we were able to get it running. The next step was changing out the fluids.

We could get it running but it didn't want to stay running. After more trouble shooting we needed to look closer at the engine. After closer inspection this motor was not salvageable. A second motor was sourced and swapped in.

The bike is currently running well. The exhaust was painted and placed back on. The next step is cleaning up paint and metal and sourcing a seat.
