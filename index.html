Welcome to Shotty Cycle Plaza

Shotty Cycle Plaza is a motorcycle mechanic shop based in Kansas City, Kansas. Owner/Founder Jordan Thomas has been repairing and modifying a wide range of motorcycles for the last two decades. Shotty Cycle Plaza provides full-service repairs, regular maintenance, and custom tuning. Our specialty is late 70s small displacement Hondas.
